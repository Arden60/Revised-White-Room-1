Process

  1. Specify the number of multi-tracks (both stereo and mono) and the length,tempo and key of each song to be mixed. Please provide as much information as you can about how you would like your music to sound. If possible, send me a rough mix (mp3 only) of the song(s) and any reference songs, such as links to YouTube, Spotify, Bandcamp, etc, specifying what aspects of each reference it is you like. This helps me to be able to see your vision more clearly.

  2. I will send you a full list of all the necessary requirements regarding the preparation of your files.

  3. Upload audio files to the Dropbox link which I will send you. Just Zip your session and put it into the Dropbox.

  4. If your session is a particularly large one, you can post a USB stick to me.

  5. Finished mixes will be uploaded as an mp3 to the Dropbox for approval.

  6. Two mix revisions are included in the price, so as to be able to get everything sitting as perfectly as possible. If you require a revision, please inform me within 48 hours of each mix delivery. Any additional revisions above two will be charged at an hourly rate.

  7. Payment via Bank transfer or PayPal. After full payment I will supply you with a 24bit 48k WAV file of each song ready for mastering.

Rates

  • Prices can vary depending on track count and song length.
