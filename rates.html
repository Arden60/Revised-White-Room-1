Rates

  • Prices are only for song lengths of 5 minutes or less. Longer songs can be negotiated.

  • 1 – 16 tracks (files) $200 AUD per song

  • 17 - 32 tracks $300 AUD per song

  • 33 – 48 tracks $400 AUD per song

  • 49 – 64 tracks $500 AUD per song

  • Over 64 tracks Contact us for pricing

Process

  1. Define the number of multi-tracks and the length and tempo of each song to be mixed. Please provide as much information as you can about how you would like your music to sound. If possible, send me a rough mix (mp3 only) of the song(s) and any reference songs, such as links to YouTube, Spotify, Bandcamp, etc, specifying what aspects of each reference it is you like. This helps me to be able to see your vision more clearly.

  2. I will send you a full list of all the necessary requirements regarding the preparation of your files.

  3. Upload audio files to the Dropbox link which I will send you. Just Zip your session and put it into the Dropbox.

  4. If your session is a particularly large one, you can post a USB stick to me.

  5. Finished mixes will be uploaded as an mp3 to the Dropbox for approval.

  6. Two mix revisions are included in the price, so as to be able to get everything sitting as perfectly as possible. If you require a revision, please inform me within 48 hours of each mix delivery. Any additional revisions above two will be charged at an hourly rate.

  7. Payment via PayPal. After full payment I will supply you with a 24bit 48k WAV file of each song ready for mastering.
