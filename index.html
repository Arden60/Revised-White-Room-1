Established in 1998 by producer Neil Coombe, The White Room is one of the longest running and most successful Queensland studios. Neil, best known for his producing, recording, engineering and mixing work on ARIA Award winning albums such as The Go-Betweens ‘Oceans Apart’ and DZ Deathrays ‘Bloodstreams’ has also worked with many other fabulous bands at The White Room such as The Grates, The Stress Of Leisure, The John Steel Singers, Robert Forster, The Good Ship, The Scare, Little Scout and Yves Klein Blue.

Using both analogue equipment and Pro Tools , along with over 25 years national and international experience, The White Room's aim is to create amazing mixes.
